Midway Atoll housing renovation (Recovery Act)

An interior view of one of the historic homes at Midway Atoll National Wildlife Refuge following the completion of renovations and energy efficiency improvements. The work was completed in December, 2010 using $398,000 in Recovery Act funding.

 

You are free to use this image with the following photo credit: U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service
